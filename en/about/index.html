About

Why human.md exists.

human.md wasn't created to sell clothes. It was created to ask a simple question. What does it mean to stay human in the age of artificial intelligence?

01

Technology changes faster than people.

Every year we build better software. Smarter systems. More capable AI. But the questions people ask remain almost the same. Who am I? What matters? What should I remember?

02

We speak the language of technology.

README. Memory. Context. Version. Branch. We borrow these words because they already describe how many of us think. But behind every technical term there is still a person.

03

human.md is for tech people.

Not only developers. Engineers. Designers. Product people. Researchers. Founders. DevRel. Security. People who spend their lives turning technology into something humans can use.

04

Objects before products.

We don't chase trends. We don't produce dozens of collections. Every release documents one idea. Every object belongs somewhere.

05

Why clothing?

Clothing is one of the few objects people carry every day. It becomes part of memory. Part of identity. Part of conversations. Sometimes a single sentence on a T-shirt says more than an entire website.

06

Why human.md?

Because documentation helps us understand complex systems. Maybe it can also help us better understand ourselves.

07

Made slowly. Made carefully.

Heavyweight cotton. Timeless silhouettes. Small collections. No unnecessary objects.

08

The future belongs to AI. Humanity belongs to us.

Technology will continue evolving. Our responsibility is different. To stay curious. To stay kind. To stay human.

Stay human.

No noise. Only meaningful updates.
